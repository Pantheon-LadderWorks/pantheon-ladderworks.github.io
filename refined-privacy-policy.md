# ReFinEd Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 8, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **IntoTheEye Ent.** ("IntoTheEye Ent.", "we", "us", or "our") collects and uses information in the **ReFinEd** application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (for authentication and communication)
- **Display name** (chosen by you)
- **Password** (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

### 2.2 Usage Data
We automatically collect information about how you use ReFinEd:
- Lessons completed and quiz scores
- Portfolio simulation activities (virtual trades)
- AI chat conversations (for improving the service)
- Login timestamps and session duration

### 2.3 Technical Information
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

### 2.4 Google OAuth Data
If you sign in with Google, we receive:
- Your Google email address
- Your Google profile name and photo (if you choose to share)
- A unique Google user ID

We do **not** access your Gmail, Google Drive, or other Google services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- **Provide the Service:** Account management, lesson tracking, portfolio simulation
- **Personalization:** Remember your progress and preferences
- **Improve ReFinEd:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **AI Features:** Process your questions to provide relevant educational responses
- **Communication:** Send important updates about the Service (with opt-out options)
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:
- **Google Firebase:** Authentication, database, and hosting
- **Google AI (Gemini):** Powers our AI Expert Chat feature

### 4.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Protect user safety

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted password storage (never plain text)
- Firestore security rules to protect user data
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Update
You can access and update your account information through your profile settings.

### 6.2 Data Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting support@refined.app. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it.

### 6.3 Opt-Out of Communications
You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email. We will still send critical account-related messages.

### 6.4 Cookie Control
You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

## 7. Children's Privacy

ReFinEd is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Users under 18 should use ReFinEd with parental supervision, as the Service contains financial education content.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using ReFinEd, you consent to such transfers.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
